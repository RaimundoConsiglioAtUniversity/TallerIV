Lost Little Horsies

A game design project by Raimundo Consiglio

Lost Little Horsies

Lost Little Horsies is a non-violent¹, 2D platforming game where a group of three horsies —a Unicorn, a Pegasus, and a Gaeahippos— try to find their way home after getting lost in early 19th century Britain.
